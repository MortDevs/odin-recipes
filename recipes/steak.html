Boneless ribeye steaks

Description

Perfectly cooked steak is a culinary masterpiece. This recipe will guide you through the steps to achieve a tender and flavorful steak, whether you prefer it rare, medium-rare, or well-done.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless ribeye steaks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 sprigs of fresh rosemary

Steps

  1. Remove the steaks from the refrigerator and let them sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat your grill or skillet to high heat.
  3. Brush the steaks with olive oil and season generously with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the steaks on the hot grill or skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare, adjusting the time for your desired level of doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, add the minced garlic and rosemary to the pan and baste the steaks with the aromatics.
  6. Remove the steaks from heat and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your perfectly cooked steak!
