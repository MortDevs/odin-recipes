Mexican Tortilla Wraps

Description

These Mexican tortilla wraps are bursting with flavors and are perfect for a quick and delicious meal. Filled with seasoned ground beef, black beans, cheese, and your favorite toppings, they're a crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream, salsa, lettuce, and tomatoes for toppings

Steps

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and water according to the seasoning packet instructions. Simmer until well combined.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a microwave or on a skillet.
  4. Assemble the tortilla wraps by placing a spoonful of the beef mixture, black beans, cheese, and your choice of toppings on each tortilla.
  5. Roll up the tortillas, tucking in the sides as you go.
  6. Serve with sour cream and salsa. Enjoy!
